# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Examples of acceptable behavior include showing empathy, respecting differing viewpoints, giving constructive feedback, and accepting responsibility. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, personal or political attacks, and publishing others' private information without permission.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban behavior they judge inappropriate, threatening, or harmful. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers will respect the reporter's privacy and respond fairly.

This code is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
